We're proud to serve those who serve others, including: individual missionaries, short-term mission groups, & anyone participating in global missions. Mission Travel provides discount international airfare for missions, churches, missionaries & humanitarians travel. Our goal is to provide you with an exceptional value and customer service experience. We focus on efficiently booking discounted airfare and minimizing mission travel expenses This maximizes your resources to impact the mission project more directly. We combine professional excellence with God's heart for mission and justice. With decades of experience in the travel industry, we want to serve those who are poor, persecuted, refugees and in need.Up and Away aims to mobilise people that have a heart for service by providing an efficient, cost effective, professional and consultative travel service.Up and Away incorporates a genuine care for people and their stories, a willingness to be open, a radical generosity and a real sense of adventure!

Our Values

Why do we do what we do?

What we do and how we do it is not what is important to us. WHY we do what we do is our greatest driving factor and it is the WHY that we as a team and business have fixed our eyes on. We want to be courageous and outrageous – to be educators, generous with our time and knowledge and to trust God for His provision.

We want to educate – we want to lighten the load of our clients - not for profits, churches, schools and individuals. We want to enable them to get on with the business of saving and transforming lives.


Values What does this mean? How does this look?
Responsiveness
  • Listens to individuals (customers, suppliers, peers)
  • Communicate openly and freely
  • Be open and available to listen and help
  • Be constructive and positive in all conversations
  • Embracing, empathetic, proactive and with genuine care
  • To look out for one another & our clients
Integrity
  • Always acts ethically
  • Tell the truth
  • Honesty and transparency
  • Learn from our mistakes to improve
Impartiality
  • Treats all individuals equitably and fairly
  • Treat others the way that we would want to be treated
  • Consistency and professionalism is key
Accountability
  • Understands requirements of own role and takes responsibility and is accountable for actions
  • Gains clarification about expected outcomes
  • Takes personal responsibility for resolving difficult problems or issues
  • Collaboration – teach & learn from one another
Respect
  • Treats all individuals with dignity and respect
  • Builds trust through consistent actions, values and communication
  • Remain calm and in control under pressure
  • Maintain a high degree of respect amongst the team & with clients
  • Still take time to have fun!
Leadership
  • Our open office environment promotes information sharing
  • Appreciate & understand different skill sets and experience within our team.
  • Shows initiative to overcome difficult or unforeseen challenges
